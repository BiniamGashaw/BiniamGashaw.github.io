About Me

As a sophomore student at the University of Nevada, Reno, pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science, my academic and professional journey is deeply rooted in a passion for technology and innovation. With a strong educational foundation from West Career and Technical Academy and W. Clark High School, where I excelled in the Program of Engineering and the Academy of Math, Science, Arts, and Technology (A.M.S.A.T.), achieving impressive GPAs of 4.1 and 5.143, my dedication to the field of computer science has been unwavering. My experience ranges from hands-on roles in customer service at Cafe Zupas and Dutch Bros, where I honed my communication and teamwork skills, to a more technical stint as a Data Entry Technician at Creative Technologies, working with LED Pixels and related devices. These roles have not only enriched my understanding of technology in practical settings but also highlighted the importance of meticulous attention to detail and efficient data handling. My proficiency in programming languages such as C++, C, and RStudio, complemented by skills in Microsoft Office Suite and Google Workspace, reflects my commitment to staying abreast of the evolving tech landscape. Active participation in extracurricular activities like the National Honor Society and varsity sports has further developed my leadership skills and team spirit. Bilingual in English and Amharic, I bring a diverse perspective to my endeavors. As a tenacious, cordial, and intuitive individual with strong deductive reasoning, I am driven to explore the depths of computer science, aiming to contribute innovative solutions in the tech world.
